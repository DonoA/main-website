API Day!

5/5/14 && 5/6/14 - 5:30-7:00 PM - Kenna 102 (Day 1), Kenna 104 (Day 2)

Come join us on either Monday or Tuesday for a guided lesson on using Ajax and JSON with APIs, with a focus on the Google Maps API. These events are completely accessible for anyone who's completed COEN 10 or CSCI 10, or with really any previous coding experience. Even those who have experience with APIs should come out and code with us! Dinner will be provided.

Freshman Day

2/22/14 - 12-5 PM - Bannnan Engineering 325 && 326

A way to expand your knowledge about programming since school classes can't teach you everything. This Event help you get your foot into the door of the programming world. There will be workshops on scripting, website development, and Vi to Vim as well as a design sprint, to help create ideas for projects, products, as well as ideas for the upcoming hackathon the week after.
