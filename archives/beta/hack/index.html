Hack for a cause



In our year of 2014, everyone is connected via the internet. Literally everyone, including the homeless. Over the past few years, technology has decreased in price value to where even homeless people can start to afford smartphones. Due to this, there is a large consumer base which many companies have not even realized exists! Sadly, there are very few applications helping out the poorer classes. Hack for the homeless is a way for us students to start making a difference in the world, one hack at a time. We are promoting helping out the homeless by encouraging you to create tools which will help them find jobs, food, shelter, the essentials in life.
